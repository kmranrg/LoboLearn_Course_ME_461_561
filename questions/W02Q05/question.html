Residual Sum of Squares

Consider the following loss function: \[ \mathcal{L}(\hat{\beta}_0,\hat{\beta}_1) \;=\; \tfrac12 \sum_{n=1}^N \bigl(y_n - (\hat{\beta}_0 + \hat{\beta}_1 \, x_n)\bigr)^2. \] Setting \(\displaystyle \frac{\partial \mathcal{L}}{\partial \hat{\beta}_1} = 0\) yields which of the following conditions?

Hint: \(\hat{y}_n = \hat{\beta}_0 + \hat{\beta}_1 \, x_n \,\); make use of algebraic trick.
